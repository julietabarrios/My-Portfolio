    Foodies Delivery Website

    My version of a food delivery website with e-commerce funcionalities. It has two possible views, one for the consumer and the other one for the partnes/restaurant. The restaurant can register, log in, add a dish, edit a dish and their information, delete a dish or their own account. Also, they have the possibility to add pictures of the restaurant and dishes due to Cloudinary. In the consumer view you will se the cart where you can add, substract or delete the whole dish and using Stripe it is possible to enter the payment details and confirm the order.

    Built with: JavaScript, React, Express, MongoDB, Stripe, Cloudinary, HTML and CSS
